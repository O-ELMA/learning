What is this place?

Clyto-Tech Control Center is a mobile base designed to welcome designated operators and provide all the paramount equipment they require for managing the Wall and its modules. Amongst other things, you have control over several billion cells that compose the barrier between you and the rest of the planet.

The aforementioned barrier is a wall that was meant to contain the island within, alongside a potential threat detected within its grounds. Both the residents of the island and the rest of the known civilisation did not know the outcome of the ongoing contagion. Extinction was presumed after the disappearance of The Well. Nevertheless, the wall was made to stop whichever threat became prominent first, protecting both sides from what resides in the other.
